• New confirmed cases: The number of new people who tested positive for the novel coronavirus according to data by the RIVM. The dashed line is the 5-day centered moving average.
    ⚠️ The number of confirmed cases depends on the testing strategy and doesn't predict future cases or the number of corona patients at the ICU.
  • Current ICU patients: Total number of (positively tested or suspected) COVID-19 patients that is currently being treated at an ICU in the Netherlands. Data for March 14, 15, 16 was recovered from various news sources. Data from March 17 to 27 was provided by the NVIC. Data starting from March 28 is provided by NICE.
  • Maximum ICU capacity (1100 to 1500): Intensive Care capacity (called ICU, or IC in Dutch) for corona patients. Current maximum IC capacity for corona patients as reported by NOS on March 29. Maximum capacity that can be offered in the near future, as reported by NOS on March 19.
  • Why this graph? Most graphs about corona show cumulative numbers (the line can only go up). Next to that, I couldn't find a graph that showed ICU numbers from the NVIC over time (the NVIC only provides the number for today on its website). I've combined RIVM and NVIC data in a graph, which I'm now sharing online.
